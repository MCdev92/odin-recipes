Chicken Burrito Protein Bowl

Description:

Mexican seasoned chicken, cilantro lime rice, guacamole, oh my! Get your healthy on with this high protein, macro-friendly, & delicious chicken burrito bowl!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 lime
  • 2 tbs cilantro
  • 12 oz chicken breast, raw weight
  • Favorite Mexican seasoning
  • ⅓ cup no salt added black beans
  • ½ cup fresh pico de gallo or salsa
  • ¼ cup guacamole
  • ¼ cup nonfat greek yogurt
  • ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce

Directions:

  1. Toss cooked white rice with chopped cilantro, squeeze of lime juice and a pinch of salt
  2. Cook chicken breast with your favorite Mexican seasoning and bake or grill as desired
  3. Cut cooked chicken into bite size pieces

Build you bowl:

  • Add cilantro Lime Rice
  • Add shredded lettuce
  • Add chicken breast
  • Add black beans
  • Add Guacamole
  • Add fresh pico de gallo
  • Add greek yogurt
  • Add a sprinkle of cheddar cheese and a wage of lime
  • Serve with chips
